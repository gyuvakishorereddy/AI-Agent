## Applicationprocess

### Steps
- Visit KARE admissions office
- Submit scholarship application form
- Provide required documents
- Wait for verification
- Scholarship approval notification

### Documents
- Income certificate
- Caste certificate (if applicable)
- Previous marks sheets
- JEE scorecard (for merit scholarship)
- Sports certificates (for sports quota)
- Aadhar card
- Bank account details
