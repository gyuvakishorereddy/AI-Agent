# Websites

## Officialwebsites

### Mainwebsite
- **Name:** Kalasalingam Academy of Research and Education (KARE)
- **Url:** https://www.kalasalingam.ac.in/
- **Description:** Main University Website - Primary portal for general information, programs offered, faculty details, news, and more
- **Purpose:** General information, programs, admissions, news, and updates

### Admissionsportal
- **Name:** Online Application Portal
- **Url:** https://apply.kalasalingam.ac.in/
- **Description:** New users registration and application portal
- **Purpose:** Apply for various programs and courses

### Applicantlogin
- **Name:** Applicant Login Portal
- **Url:** https://kalvi.kalasalingam.ac.in/klustudentportal
- **Description:** Existing users login to complete application or track status
- **Purpose:** Complete applications and track application status

### Admissionsinfo
- **Name:** Admissions Information
- **Url:** https://www.kalasalingam.ac.in/admissions/
- **Description:** Overview of programs, eligibility, fee structure, and scholarships
- **Purpose:** Detailed admissions information and requirements

### Studentinformation
- **Name:** Student Information System (KARE-SIS)
- **Url:** https://sis.kalasalingam.ac.in/
- **Description:** Student portal for academic records, registration, and results
- **Purpose:** Access grades, attendance, course registration, exam schedules, fee payment, and academic records

### Learningmanagement
- **Name:** Learning Management System (LMS)
- **Url:** https://lms.kalasalingam.ac.in/
- **Description:** Online learning resources, course materials, and assignments
- **Purpose:** Access course materials, assignments, and online learning resources

### Hostelportal
- **Name:** Hostel Booking Portal
- **Url:** https://hostels.kalasalingam.ac.in
- **Description:** Portal for booking hostel accommodation
- **Purpose:** Hostel room booking, hostel fee payment, room allocation, and hostel-related information

### Contactpage
- **Name:** Contact Us
- **Url:** https://www.kalasalingam.ac.in/contact/
- **Description:** Phone numbers, email addresses, and physical address of the university
- **Purpose:** Contact information and university address details

## Importantnotes
- **Universityname:** Kalasalingam Academy of Research and Education (KARE) - formerly Kalasalingam University
- **Logincredentials:** Use your student ID and password to access SIS, LMS, and Hostel portals
- **Support:** Contact IT helpdesk for login issues or technical support
- **Mobileaccess:** All portals are mobile-friendly and can be accessed from smartphones
- **Resultsaccess:** Academic results are typically available through the Student Information System (KARE-SIS)
- **Examinfo:** For examination schedules and detailed results, navigate to 'Controller of Examinations (CoE)' or 'Academics' sections on the main website
- **Currentinfo:** For the most current information on academic calendars and examination schedules, always check the main website

## Categories
- **Generalinfo:** Official website and contact information
- **Admissions:** Application portals and admission-related information
- **Studentportals:** Academic and learning management systems for enrolled students
- **Hostel:** Accommodation booking and management
- **Examination:** Results and examination-related information available through SIS and main website
