## Documentsubmissionguidelines

### Importantinstructions
- All documents must be submitted at the time of joining college
- Original documents are required for verification
- Xerox copies must be clear and legible
- Community certificate required only for BC/MBC/SC/ST students
- Entrance exam scorecards required if applicable
- Medical fitness certificate must be from a registered medical practitioner

### Photospecifications

#### Passportsize
- **Quantity:** 4
- **Requirements:** Recent passport size photographs with clear face visibility

#### Familyphoto
- **Quantity:** 2
- **Size:** Postcard size
- **Requirements:** Family photograph including student

### Statespecificrequirements

#### Tamilnadu
- **Requirement:** 11th and 12th mark sheets for TN State Board students

#### Andhratelangana
- **Requirement:** Intermediate Mark Statement with both Short Memo and Long Memo

### Checklist

#### Beforesubmission
- Verify all documents are original with required xerox copies
- Check photograph quality and quantity
- Ensure Aadhar card xerox is clear
- Get medical fitness certificate from registered doctor
- Arrange community certificate if applicable
- Keep entrance exam scorecards ready
